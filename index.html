Frequently Asked Questions

How does the spin wheel work?

Our spin wheel uses JavaScript to create a realistic spinning animation. When you click the spin button, the wheel rotates and randomly stops on one of the options. The result is determined by the angle at which the wheel stops.

Can I customize the wheel options?

Yes, you can fully customize the wheel options. Simply enter your desired options in the text area provided, separated by commas, and click the "Update Wheel" button to apply the changes.

Is my data stored when I use the wheel?

No, we don't store any of your data. The wheel operates entirely in your browser, and any customizations you make are temporarily stored in your browser's local storage for convenience but are not sent to any server.

Can I use the spin wheel on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our spin wheel is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The touch interface is optimized for mobile use.

Is there a limit to how many options I can add?

While there's no strict limit, we recommend keeping the number of options between 2 and 12 for the best visual experience. Too many options can make the wheel sections very small and difficult to read.
